# Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your BrigadeMate account and associated data directly in the app:

Menu > Account > Delete Account

If you cannot access the app, contact us at:

Email: brigademate@gmail.com

Please include the email address of your account and the phrase "Account deletion request" in the subject line.

We will verify your request and delete your account data within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law.
